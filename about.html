Our Heritage.

Established in 1997, Flourish Paper & Chemicals Limited has seen an exhilarating journey from trading of Chemicals to Manufacturing of Sizing Chemicals for Paper Industry & now Agro Chemicals Intermediates, Recoveries of Acids & Solvent Recovery Plant. Company has reinvented itself in 2019, from Sizing Chemicals to Intermediates with all state of art utilities consisting of 14 reactors with a mix of Glass Lined and Stainless Steel fitted with Glass Lined, Graphite & Stainless Steel Columns & condensers, Boiler, ETP, Chiller, Brine, MEE, Multimill, ANFD to handle any type of Product, spread over 15 Acres with storage of 2000 MT of Liquid Chemicals Our Company and our team have merited the trust and loyalty of our clients by ensuring - consistent quality products, constant supply, up-to-date service, competitive pricing & in house Transportation consisting of 12 Specialised Vehicles to ensure timely deliveries. FPCL aims to be the leader in the chosen field of chemicals. We aspire to add power and value to our consumers, our partners, and our employees.
